# Research Workspace

This directory is the working skeleton for the personal research operating system.

## Top-Level Structure

- `00_inbox/`: temporary intake and unsorted materials
- `01_literature/`: literature-side workbench, not the Zotero master library
- `02_notes/`: Obsidian-oriented research knowledge base
- `03_methods/`: method learning, toy examples, and reusable scripts
- `04_data/`: raw, interim, and processed datasets
- `05_projects/`: concrete research or learning projects
- `06_outputs/`: shareable memos, reports, figures, and exports
- `07_admin/`: plans, task lists, and lightweight management files
- `99_archive/`: inactive or historical materials
